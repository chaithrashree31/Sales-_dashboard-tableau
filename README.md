# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)
![Tableau Dashboard](Snapshot%20of%20the%20Dahboard.png)
## 📊 Project Overview
This project is an interactive Tableau dashboard providing real-time visual analysis of multi-category retail performance. It is designed to track year-over-year (YoY) sales health and identify key drivers in product categories, consumer segments, and seasonal trends.

## 🎯 Primary Purpose
To enable rapid sales analysis for retail managers by answering:
* How does current performance ($746K) compare to the previous year ($614K)?
* Which business segments (Consumer vs. Corporate) are driving volume?
* What specific products are the top profit generators?

## ✨ Features & Visual Highlights
* **KPI Summary (Left Panel):** Instant YoY comparison of total sales and total orders (1,053).
* **Category Analysis:** Horizontal bar chart isolating Furniture ($172K), Technology ($163K), and Office Supplies ($138K) performance.
* **Donut Chart Segment Breakdown:** A visual breakdown of Corporate ($129K) vs. Consumer ($267K) vs. Home Office ($77K) customer segments.
* **Seasonal Area Chart:** A **Monthly Trend** showing peak periods (e.g., the significant jump to $76K in November).
* **Product Deep Dive:** Ranked list of **Top 5 Selling Products** (e.g., Fellowes PB500).

## 💡 Business Insights
* **Q4 Dominance:** Sales volume is significantly weighted towards November/December, driven by holiday demand.
* **High-Volume, Low-Margin Risk:** Product deep dive reveals high-selling binders; margin analysis (if linked) would confirm profitability.
* **Segmentation Priority:** The Consumer segment is the largest driver, accounting for 56% of total sales.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Tableau Desktop:** For data visualization.
* **Data Source:** SQL Database / Excel (e.g., "Sample - Superstore" data).
